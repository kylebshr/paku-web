Developer: Paku is developed by Kyle Bashour, under the guise of Pitou Technologies, LLC

5.0 Launch

Paku 5 is launching in Fall 2023 alongside iOS 17, macOS 14 and watchOS 10. This is Paku’s biggest update yet, with a brand new design, gorgeous new widgets, and tons of new features and improvements.

What’s New

→ Brand new design with improved search, favorites, trends, and charts.
→ View beautiful charts for every data point, and select one week, three days, or 24 hours of history.
→ Configure Paku to show the details you care about; data points can be hidden, and charts toggled on or off.
→ Favorites and other settings now sync to all your devices through iCloud.
→ Improved EPA guidance and callouts for sensor status.

Widgets

→ Gorgeous new chart widgets allow you to view historical AQI, temperature, humidity, or raw particulate values (requires Paku Pro).
→ New “Big Stat” widget for easy glanceability.
→ Support for Lock Screen widgets on iPadOS.
→ Support for Desktop widgets on macOS.
→ Support for the watchOS 10 Smart Stack.
→ Improvements to Smart Select, which filters out unreliable sensors from the Nearby source.

Alerts

→ Improvement Alerts are now available, to let you know when the AQI has improved below your threshold.
    → These will be enabled by default for new alerts; you can manually enable them for existing ones.
→ Improved alert configuration with a dedicated settings screen.
→ You can now choose a custom AQI threshold for alerts.

Favorites

→ Favorites can now be configured to show specific data points.
→ Favorites can now be reordered by tapping the “Edit” button.
→ You can now view the nearest sensor at the top of your favorites.

watchOS

→ Brand new watchOS 10 app design.
→ View data for your favorite sensors right from the sensor list, and view charts in the sensor details.
→ Favorites now sync from with your phone.
