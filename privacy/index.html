Paku Privacy Policy

Personal Information

Paku’s app developer cares deeply about your privacy. Besides the data that Apple provides to developers, Paku doesn’t collect any personal information and uses no analytics frameworks.

Location

Paku uses the location services of your device to determine which AQI values to display. The location data never leaves your device.

Purple Air

Paku uses Purple Air sensor data. By using Paku, you are subject to Purple Air’s Privacy Policy. Your location is never sent to Purple Air.

Updates

This policy may be updated at any time. You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

Contact

If you have questions or complaints concerning your data or this privacy policy, please email support@paku.app.
